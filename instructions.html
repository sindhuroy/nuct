NUCT 2021

THIS NUCT {NSUT UNOFFICIAL CRICKET TOURNAMENT }, ORGANISED BY STUDENTS FOR THE STUDENTS


1. Eligibility: 1st year students who are registered to our NUCT.
2. NUCT will began from 22nd APRIL
Duration is dependent on no. Of Teams
Timing of the matches would be fixed when both teams are comfortable! And its dependent on the class time table of teams.
3. Format of the tournament will be a mix of league and knockouts (more information will be shared through captain).
4. Format of the match:
Match will be of 10overs per innings. Only one bowler is allowed to bowl 3 overs and for rest maximum allowance will be of 2 overs.
About powerplay
There will be a powerplay from 1st to 3rd over.
In powerplay - maximum 2 fielders outside the circle.
Without powerplay- maximum 5 fielders outside the circle.
5. Equipments
Bat has to be brought by respective teams
Ball has been decided and will be provided by us for each innigng on the match day.
We need some volunteers who can provide stumps for smooth conduction of NUCT (volunteers please dm us )
6. Umpiring
The umpiring would be done by the admins , none of the umpire would be from the section playing in match, for fare gameplay.
There would be 2 umpires.
7. SELECTION OF TEAM
We NUCT do not interfere your team selection, Apne hi Section k khelne wale bache selection process karwaenge .
Only one team is allowed from each section
SO, choose your best playing 11(min.) - 15 (max.)
8. Prize ka scene -
Its non- profit , unofficially organised for the students by the students.
We NUCT do not hold any type of profit by any means/
Remember - its a way for recreational, joy with all the NSUTians.
